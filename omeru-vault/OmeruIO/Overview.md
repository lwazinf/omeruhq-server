# OmeruIO — Public Storefront

The customer-facing storefront platform. Every merchant gets a store at `omeru.io/@handle`.

## What lives here
- Store pages (`/app/[locale]/[handle]/`)
- Invite/apply flow (`/apply`)
- Privacy, legal pages
- Multilingual: English (default), Afrikaans, Zulu
